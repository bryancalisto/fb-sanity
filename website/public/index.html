FB Sanity for Chrome

Hide the noise on Facebook.

Looking at the Facebook reactions, the 👍❤️😂😡 counters, can trigger a nerve for some people. They can be hard to ignore, and they may affect you in ways that neither psychologists nor sociologists can truly measure.

Simple controls Toggle the reaction summary bar and/or the full reactions container visibility from the popup.
No user data The extension does not collect any data from you.
Runs on Facebook The extension code activates only when visiting facebook.com website.
Kept minimal and simple No bloathing features. Just does the hiding / displaying.
