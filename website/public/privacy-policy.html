FB Sanity - Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-22

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the FB Sanity Chrome extension (the "Extension") handles information when you use it.

FB Sanity is designed to hide Facebook reaction UI elements on facebook.com and www.facebook.com. The Extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information or user content through the Extension.

No user data is used by the Extension.

FB Sanity does not track browsing history, read page content for analytics, collect identifiers, or send any data to external servers.

The Extension only stores your local preference settings, such as whether you want to hide reaction bars or full reaction containers. These settings are stored in Chrome's extension storage so the Extension can remember your choices.

3. How We Use Information

Because the Extension does not collect user data, there is no personal data to use for analytics, advertising, profiling, or resale.

The only stored information is your extension preferences, which are used to apply your chosen behavior when Facebook pages load.

4. Storage and Retention

FB Sanity uses chrome.storage to save your extension preferences locally in your Chrome profile. This storage is used only to remember your settings across browser sessions.

These preferences remain on your device until you change them or remove the Extension. They are not used to identify you and are not transmitted to us or to third parties.

5. Permissions and Host Access

The Extension requests only the permissions required to function as intended.

  • storage - required by Google Chrome so the Extension can save your local settings.
  • matches for facebook.com and www.facebook.com - used so the Chrome extension code runs when you visit those Facebook pages.

These permissions are used solely to enable the Extension's core feature. They are not used to collect or transmit user data.

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not rely on third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external data processors.

Chrome itself provides the extension platform and local storage APIs used by the Extension. Google may process extension data according to its own Chrome policies and platform operations, but FB Sanity does not send user data to any separate service.

7. Your Choices

  • You can change the extension settings at any time from the popup UI.
  • You can remove the Extension from Chrome to delete its locally stored preferences.
  • You can block or limit the Extension by disabling it in Chrome's extension manager.

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the extension maintainer through the project page or repository associated with FB Sanity.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 2026-05-22.

This policy is specific to the FB Sanity Chrome extension.
